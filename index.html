Raising Awareness About Vaping

What is Vape?


Indeed everything nowadays are upgrading even on smoking, from cigarretes to e-cigarettes or what we called vape. According to TeensHealth.org, vape is a battery-operated device that allow users to inhale vapor infused with nicotine and flavorings. Vape comes in a variety of flavors and it also go by different names, including vapes, vape pens or sticks, pods, or dispo.

It also considered hazardous waste because of its nicotine and battery content. The Environmental Protection Agency (the EPA) considers liquid nicotine “acute hazardous waste.”

It is important to be informed about vaping as well as its negative effects on one’s health. Making good and educated decisions about health so that the youth, the people can be aware of the risks connected to vaping.

It begins with you, don't let vaping ruin your life. Vaping has become an alternative to traditional cigarettes. Most people, especially young ones who do not smoke are influenced to use it without considering its potential health risk. It attracts them to use vape because they think it is cool and safe to use. That is why it is important to be aware about what vaping truly is. Every decision matters. Be smart and choose not to vape.

Flavored vape leads to flavored air, so don't be a cloud chaser, but be a dream chaser instead.
